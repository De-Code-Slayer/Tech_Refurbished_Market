general faq’s

What's the difference between a used iPhone and a refurbished iPhone?

In both cases, the devices are pre-owned. The similarities end there. A refurbished iPhone has been tuned up and brought back to perfect working condition in a factory before being sold on Tech Refurbished. So basically a refurbished phone has the double advantage of being a inexpensive smartphone while also having a professional refurbisher guarantee its excellent quality.

How do I know that my refurbished tablet isn't going to conk out in 1 month?

You have our good faith, but we know that's not enough. If you run into an unlikely glitch, your device is covered under warranty for 1 year and you can exchange or return your item within the first 30 days. But don't take our word for it, check our customer reviews.

What battery life level should I receive my device with?

If your device's battery health is above 85% within one month of purchase, or 80% within the first 6 months, the state of its battery complies with our Quality Charter. This applies to smartphones purchased in all aesthetic conditions. After six months of purchase, battery health issues are not covered by the warranty, as it's normal for batteries to degrade when used. Please contact Tech Refurbished Customer Care if your device’s battery is below the percentages above when you receive it.

Can I checkout as a guest?

We ask all potential Buyers (that's you, dear customer) to create an account to place an order. Why? 'cause we've got your back. With a Tech Refurbished account, you can track the device you just ordered to make sure it's getting to you safe and sound; you can also contact the refurbisher directly if your phone is taking too dang long (or with literally any questions you have about your order). You can also hit us up (Tech Refurbished) too! You're not a guest, you're family—an account ensures you're getting the best of the best as a Backer. Get the scoop on accounts
